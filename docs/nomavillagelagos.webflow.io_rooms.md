# Private Rooms

More than just a place to sleep

Welcome to your own little private oasis at our Coliving & Coworking Space! Our rooms are designed with your comfort and privacy in mind. Each room features its own private bathroom, so you can enjoy your personal space without any interruptions.

Feel right at home and start your day feeling refreshed and rejuvenated. Our rooms are tastefully decorated with a welcoming ambiance. You'll find yourself sinking into a big-sized, super comfortable bed, ensuring a restful night's sleep. Whether you have important calls to make or need to focus on work, our rooms also provide a convenient little desk for you to utilize.

Natural daylight fills the space through large sliding doors, creating a bright and light-flooded environment. Step outside and you'll find a cozy patio, complete with extra chairs and a small table (most rooms). It's the perfect spot to unwind and soak up the tranquil surroundings.
